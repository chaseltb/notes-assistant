# Cellular Respiration

## Glycolysis
Glycolysis occurs in the cytoplasm and breaks down glucose into pyruvate, producing 2 ATP and 2 NADH.

## Krebs Cycle
The Krebs cycle (citric acid cycle) takes place in the mitochondrial matrix. Each acetyl-CoA produces 3 NADH, 1 FADH2, and 1 ATP.

## Oxidative Phosphorylation
The electron transport chain uses NADH and FADH2 to pump protons across the inner mitochondrial membrane, driving ATP synthase to produce ~32 ATP.
