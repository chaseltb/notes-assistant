# Biology - Cell Respiration

## Glycolysis
Glycolysis occurs in the cytoplasm. One glucose molecule yields 2 ATP and 2 NADH.

## Krebs Cycle
The Krebs cycle occurs in the mitochondrial matrix and produces 2 ATP, 6 NADH, 2 FADH2 per glucose.

## Oxidative Phosphorylation
Occurs on the inner mitochondrial membrane. Produces approximately 32-34 ATP via the electron transport chain.

## Summary
Total yield: 36-38 ATP per glucose molecule.
